Our 7-Tank Powder Coating Process

1 Alkaline Cleaning

Removes oils, grease, and surface contaminants for perfect adhesion.

2 Rinse

Thorough water rinse removes remaining cleaning solution.

3 Acid Etching

Removes light rust and oxidation while micro-etching the surface.

4 Rinse

Removes all acid residues for a perfectly clean surface.

5 Phosphating

Creates corrosion-resistant base layer for enhanced durability.

6 Final Rinse

Deionized water rinse leaves ultra-clean surface ready for coating.

7 Chromate Rinse

Optional chromate rinse provides additional corrosion protection.
